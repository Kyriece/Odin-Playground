Lasagna!

Description

This is Kyriece's famous lasanga, made with alot of meat, cheese, pasta and love. While
lasanga always takes a while to make, its well worth the trouble when it comes to this recipe.

Ingredients

For the mince

  • 800g of crushed tomatoes
  • 1kg of bolognese mince (pork + beef)
  • 2 onion, finely diced
  • 4 table spoons of minced garlic
  • half a jar of tomato paste
  • olive oil
  • 500g chicken stock

For the Lasanga

  • A bagful of cheese (mozeralla, parmasan, and tasty cheese are good)
  • 5 layers worth of sheets sized to your favourite lasanga pan

For the white sauce

Makes 1 cup worth

  • 2 Tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1¼ cups whole milk, heated
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

Bolognese first!

  1. Dice onions finely and add to heated, oiled pan. Add 2tbsp minced garlic and cook for approx 3 minutes.
  2. Add minced meat and 2 tbsp of minced garlic and cook until almost brown.
  3. Add half jar of tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes or until meat is brown.
  4. Now add crushed tomatoes and chicken stock. Simmer for 40 minutes or until desired consistency

Make the white sauce and ready the pasta.

  1. Boil the pasta sheets for half the time recommended on the package. Remove from boil and icebath them. Afterwards place on towel to dry
  2. Warm the milk on low heat until little bubbles form at the edges; remove from heat.
  3. Melt 2 Tbsp. unsalted butter in a heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat. Stir in 2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour and cook, stirring constantly, until the paste cooks and bubbles a bit, but don't let it brown, about 2 minutes. Slowly add 1¼ cups whole milk, heated, whisking to incorporate. Bring it to a boil, stirring constantly, as sauce thickens. Add kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Reduce to low heat, and cook, stirring, 2–3 minutes more. Remove from the heat.

Construction!

  1. First add a thin layer of bolognese to the pan, then alternate layers between whitesauce + Bolognese, cheese, and pasta sheet. Cook for 1hr, 180 Degrees Celcius
  2. Enjoy!
